Jade Parcels

An exquisite-looking dish.

Description: The ham's sweetness is locked inside the fresh vegetables, drizzled with a spicy broth. Delicious is an understatement.

Effect: Increases all party members' ATK by 272 and CRIT Rate by 8% for 300s.

Type: ATK-Boosting Dishes

Depending on the quality, Jade Parcels increases the party's attack by 224/272/320 and critical rate by 6/8/10% for 300 seconds. Like most foods, this has no effect for other players in Co-Op Mode.

Ingredients

  • 3 Lotus Head
  • 2 Jueyun Chili
  • 2 Cabbage
  • 1 Ham

Steps

  1. Find a campfire or a stove and click on it.
  2. Choose the Jade Parcels dish.
  3. Click the screen in the right time to get the Jade Parcels Delicious Dish.
  4. Claim it and give it to your characters.
