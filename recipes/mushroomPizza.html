Mushroom Pizza

A pizza covered in cheese and mushrooms.

Description: When still warm the cheese stretches out long, making it extremely satisfying to eat.

Effect: Restores 28% of Max HP to the selected character. Regenerates 620 HP every 5s for 30s.

Type: Recovery Dishes

Depending on the quality, Mushroom Pizza restores 26/28/30% of Max HP to the selected character, and regenerates 450/620/790 HP every 5 seconds for 30 seconds. Like most foods, this has no effect for other players in Co-Op Mode.
