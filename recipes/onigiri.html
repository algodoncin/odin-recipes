Onigiri

A portable dish made with rice.

Description: It is simple to make and remains delicious at room temperatures, which has made it very popular with civilians and soldiers alike.

Effect: Restores 20% of Max HP to the selected character and regenerates 390 HP every 5s for 30s.

Type: Recovery Dishes

Ingredients

  • 2 Fish
  • 1 Rice
  • 2 Seagrass

Steps

  1. Find a campfire or a stove and click on it.
  2. Choose the Onigiri dish.
  3. Click the screen in the right time to get the Onigiri Delicious Dish.
  4. Claim it and give it to your characters.
